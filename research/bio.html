Bio

I am currently a full professor at University of Rennes 2 since September 2022, following nine years as an assistant professor. I conduct research in the LETG laboratory, with a focus on machine learning and indexing for time series data, specifically for environmental time series. I am also an associate member of the Obelix group at IRISA.
